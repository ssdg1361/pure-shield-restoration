A hot water leak from the condo above caused mold to grow behind the drywall in this **Boca Raton** condo. By the time it surfaced on the walls, the moisture had already spread behind them.

## How we handled it
We set up full containment and ran **HEPA air scrubbers** to keep spores from drifting into the rest of the unit, then safely removed the affected drywall and materials, treated the area, and restored it. We checked moisture levels throughout to make sure nothing was left growing behind the walls.

## The result
The condo was cleared, cleaned, and restored — with the moisture source addressed so the mold doesn't come back.
